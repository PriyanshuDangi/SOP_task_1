How To Play

INSTRUCTIONS

This is a fun bubble burst game in which bubbles appear at random places on the screen. The main aim of the player is to burst the bubbles before they fill up a major part of the screen by tapping over the bubbles.If major part of screen is covered with bubbles for 10 seconds, the game ends. A timer will also be shown.

Bubbles

All the randomlycolor changing bubble will be burst on one tap and will add one points to your score. There will be rock bubble which will be of grey color and will not change its colors. There eill be three dots over it and it will burst over 5 taps and will add 10 points to your total score.

PowerUps

1. Felix-Felicis

This will be present on the top right corner of the screen.This slows down the rate of generation of bubbles for sometime. You can only use this twice throughout the game.

2. Gauntlet

This will come rarely on-screen on clicking which you make a snap and clear half of the screen at once . It will be present at the top center.
